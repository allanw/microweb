About

Create a new membership group

A membership group is a group of people that have collectively been granted some permissions.

By default a person has no permission to do anything at all, and permission is granted through membership of one or more membership groups.

To create a semi-private forum (not indexable by Google or visible to those not signed-in) do not create any membership group that contains guests and has "read" permission. But do create a membership group that contains signed-in users and has "read" permission.

To create a truly private forum, do not add guests or signed-in users, and rely solely on the criteria and named individuals to grant permissions.
